Required external packages are HDF5.  Optional external packages include
the following:

\begin{tabular}{|lll|} \hline
\textbf{Package} & \textbf{ifdef} & \textbf{description} \\ \hline
\code{lcaperf} & 
\code{USE\_LCAPERF} & 
performance toolkit  \\
\code{PAPI} & 
\code{USE\_PAPI} & 
performance API  \\
\code{hypre} & 
\code{USE\_HYPRE} & 
scalable linear solvers  \\
\code{P3DFFT} & 
\code{USE\_P3DFFT} & 
parallel 3D FFT \\
\code{arprec} & 
\code{USE\_ARPREC} & 
arbitrary precision package \\ \hline
\end{tabular}
